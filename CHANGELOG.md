## [1.0.0]

Applied stylings for:

- Headers: 1., 1.1., 1.1.1. (- as an alternative to .)
- Quotes: Texts between " "
- Parentheses notes: Texts between ( )
- List subjects: Texts between - and : (at beginning of a new line)
